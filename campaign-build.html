Visibility & Insight

How you see it working

TIER 01

DemandSense — live

Always-on dashboards: accounts reached, engaged accounts, audience tuning, in real time.

TIER 02

Monthly insights

Channel-by-channel analysis, what is working, where reserve is deployed, what changes next.

TIER 03

Impact Report (~Day 60)

The headliner. Five diagnostic layers, not a dashboard.

Re-run competitive white space. Has the field shifted? Is the wedge still uncontested?
Analyze data by persona. Which roles, verticals, sizes convert — and which to cut.
Identify visitors & prospects. Named accounts engaging via DemandSense + RB2B, handed to BDRs.
Messaging angles that worked vs. didn’t. Which pillar earned the click; recut creative.

Graduation — Ramp → Reach

UnderDefense sits in Ramp: the offer is validated, but the paid demand engine is the missing piece. This 90 days builds that engine. We graduate to Reach (demand generation to out-of-market buyers, channel expansion) when the triggers fire:

— Closed-loop attribution live (LinkedIn/Google → HubSpot, target 90%+ accuracy)   — Warm pools activated and addressable   — Qualified HubSpot MQLs from paid (reversing the prior “trash leads” problem)   — Cost-per-MQL baseline established   — Paid-influenced pipeline in HubSpot. Numeric targets to be set at the June 22 kickoff; baselines established in Month 1.
