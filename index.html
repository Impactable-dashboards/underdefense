The strategy, at a glance

The Play

Core Positioning

An agentic AI SOC backed by analysts who actually contain the threat, with incident response and compliance included and pricing you can see, proven by six years and zero ransomware across 500+ clients.

WHO WE TARGET

Security leaders and the SecOps Directors who recommend up, at 500–10K-employee US firms in Tech/SaaS, Financial Services, and Healthcare. Warm data first, then cold where the wedge is sharpest.

THE FUNNEL

Trust before the ask. Warm audiences move first; cold earns progression through engagement. We never ask a stranger for a demo.

THE OFFER

A free Security Posture Assessment paired with the public SOC Cost Calculator. Low friction, high value, a natural opener for sales.

HOW WE TRACK IT

Closed-loop attribution into HubSpot, server-side, judged on MQLs not platform conversions. Full Impact Report at ~60 days.

The wedge. The category is saturated on the same three claims — agentic AI, vendor-agnostic, transparency. The uncontested lane is what competitors hide: a price you can see, outcomes you own (humans contain), IR and compliance included, and proof you can check. That throughline runs through every module in this room.
