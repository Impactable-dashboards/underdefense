The Four Pillars

Each pillar is built from a white-space gap — the things competitors hide or structurally cannot match. The bar shows how exposed competitors are on each: the longer it is, the more open the lane. Pricing transparency is real but sits below the pillars, as commercial honesty at the close.

1 · Your SOC, Inside Your Walls

owns: the sovereignty gap (0 of 6)

SaaS, hybrid, or air-gapped on-prem — your data never leaves your boundary. Every rival AI SOC routes telemetry to their cloud. The one lane competitors structurally cannot copy.

2 · Outcomes, Not Tickets

owns: the detect-and-escalate flaw

Rivals send tickets; the customer still does the containment. Our analysts contain and verify via ChatOps.

3 · Compliance & IR, Included

owns: the unbundling gap

SOC 2 / ISO / HIPAA / PCI evidence as a byproduct + a 120-hour IR retainer in the core package. Pairs with sovereignty for the regulated buyer.

4 · Proven, Not Promised

owns: proof gap in an AI-hype market

Six years, zero ransomware, 500+ clients, 1% churn. Every entrant claims AI; few can claim outcomes.

The test for every message: if a competitor could say the same thing and it would still be true, it is not positioning. It is table stakes. Sovereignty passes that test where pricing does not, a rival can match a price overnight, but not re-architect to air-gapped.

03 / Funnel

Message Progression by Stage

Trust before the ask. Warm moves first; cold earns progression. No cold audience gets a conversion offer before it warms.

Unaware → Aware

COLD · earn attention

Problem-framing and pattern interrupts: “your AI SOC ships your data to their cloud,” tickets vs outcomes, AI hype vs proof. Reframe the problem; do not pitch.

FORMATS: Founder TLAs (video + text), single image, short video. No case studies, no demo CTAs.

Aware → Engaged

WARM RETARGETING · build credibility

Proof-first: named case studies, zero-ransomware stat, Gartner/G2, 2-minute triage. Where most conversions are set up.

FORMATS: Document Ads, case-study images, founder/customer video, cheap text for reach. No hard sells.

Engaged → Converting

HIGH-INTENT · make the ask

Specific, low-friction offer: security posture assessment, SOC cost calculator, or demo. Warm + high-intent only.

FORMATS: Lead Gen Forms (4 fields max), Conversation Ads, dedicated landing pages.

In-Pipeline → Closed

DEAL ACCELERATION · remove friction

Remove late-stage objections: deployment choice (SaaS, hybrid, or air-gapped on-prem), onboarding ease (runs on your stack), risk removal (IR included, no lock-in, all-inclusive pricing).

FORMATS: Spotlight + text ads to open-opportunity committees.
