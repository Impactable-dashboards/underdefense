03 / The Open Lane

What Nobody Is Saying

The same axes, inverted: the territory almost no competitor occupies. Each is a message UnderDefense can credibly own.

Your SOC, inside your walls

0 of 6 offer air-gapped on-prem · OWN IT

SaaS, hybrid, or full air-gapped on-prem — your raw telemetry never crosses your boundary. Every rival AI SOC requires you to send data to their cloud. The one thing competitors structurally cannot match.

Compliance as a byproduct

0 of 6 bundle compliance

SOC 2 / ISO 27001 / HIPAA / PCI evidence generated continuously from the same workflows. Pairs with sovereignty to win the regulated buyer: finance, healthcare, government, critical infrastructure.

Outcomes, not tickets

The category’s structural flaw

Analysts contain and verify via ChatOps. Everyone else detects and escalates the work back to your team.

Proven, not promised

Unmatched in an AI-hype market

Six years, zero ransomware, 500+ clients, 1% churn. Every entrant claims AI; few can claim outcomes. IR retainer included, never a separate line item.

The wedge

Your own AI-driven SOC, inside your walls, under your control. While the field competes on the same saturated AI claims and every rival routes your telemetry to their cloud, UnderDefense owns the one lane a regulated CISO cannot get anywhere else: deployment sovereignty, with humans who contain the threat, compliance evidence as a byproduct, and six years of proof. A competitor can drop its price overnight; it cannot re-architect to air-gapped. Pricing transparency rides along as commercial honesty at the close, not the hook.
